About

Netcat.us is an independent, open source, penetration testing information aggregator designed to utilize native Linux tools and networking capabilities to help red teams simplify the amount of data they need to process during engagements. Netcat.us utilizes Python, SQL, and HTML/CSS to provide instant visual categorization and feedback on data sent to it through the simple command line tool known as netcat.


Usage

echo secretkey/directory/ | cat - nmap.txt | nc netcat.us 9000
